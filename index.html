Common Questions

What is your standard revision policy?
We include one round of comprehensive revisions (editing text, font choices, or minor positioning adjustments) free of charge. Major structural changes (e.g., changing the translation style from localization to literal mid-project) may incur a small hourly fee.
How do you handle Japanese SFX (Sound Effects)?
For "Full Production" requests, we redraw background art to replace Japanese SFX with English equivalents using context-appropriate fonts. For "Translation Only" requests, we provide SFX translations in a side-note column or overlay text layer.
Do you accept raws with existing cleaning/typesetting?
Yes. If you already have cleaned pages (CLRD), we can provide the Typesetting (TS) script or place the text directly. This often reduces the cost per page.
What is your policy on file destruction/retention?
Security is paramount. All source files, raw scans, and scripts are permanently deleted from our servers 14 days after final delivery and payment confirmation, unless an extended archive period is requested.

Standard Terms of Service

1. Payment Terms

  • Standard Projects: 50% upfront deposit required to commence work. Remaining 50% due upon delivery of watermarked final files.
  • Bulk/Series: Monthly billing or milestone-based payments available for established groups.

2. Definition of "Full Production"

  • Includes: Translation, Proofreading, Cleaning (Leveling/Denoising), and Typesetting.
  • Includes: Redrawing of text over backgrounds.
  • Excludes: Complex pixel-perfect repainting of intricate art covered by large SFX (unless specifically quoted).

3. Revisions & Cancellations

  • One round of revisions is included. Subsequent major changes due to client preference shifts are billed hourly.
  • Cancellations after work has commenced result in forfeiture of the deposit to cover labor hours already spent.
