01. Scope of Service

BlackMargin Translations provides translation, editing, cleaning, typesetting, and related production services for manga, comics, doujinshi, webtoons, novels, and similar media.

1.1 Included in “Full Production”

  • Translation (JP → EN / CN → EN / etc.)
  • Proofreading & natural-flow editing
  • Cleaning (leveling, denoising, artifact removal)
  • Standard redrawing for text areas
  • Typesetting, formatting, and font selection

1.2 Not Included (Unless Quoted Separately)

  • Heavy artistic reconstruction
  • Pixel-perfect restoration behind complex SFX
  • Repainting or illustration unrelated to text removal
  • Layout redesign or multi-language edition creation
  • AI-specific prompting or custom workflow setups

The Studio may decline tasks outside this scope.
